About Me

I've lived most of my adult life in Utah, with the exception of living in the New Mexico Four Corners area for about 4 1/2 years while working for the University of Utah on a project on the Navajo Reservation. As of next year, I will have worked for the U for the past 20 years. For most of that time, I've worked in health research in various positions.

When I'm not working, I love to play tennis and travel whenever I get the chance. In the last 2 years, I've had the chance to travel in Southeast Asia in Thailand, Cambodia and Veitnam and took an amazing trip to the northern part of India. Some places still on my bucket list include: Africa, Australia/New Zealand and Greece.
